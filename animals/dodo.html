The Dodo

Welcome to our exhibit on the Dodo, an extinct bird that was once found exclusively on the island of Mauritius. Despite its name, the Dodo was a fascinating and unique creature that was more closely related to pigeons than to other birds. Unfortunately, the Dodo was hunted and introduced predators like rats and pigs led to its extinction by the late 17th century. Our exhibit showcases the history and biology of the Dodo, as well as its role in the ecological and cultural history of Mauritius. We hope you enjoy learning more about this remarkable and tragic species.
